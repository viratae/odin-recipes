Spicy Vodka

Creamy, white, and a pasta delight!

Creamy penne alfredo decorated with parmesan and delicious spinach and cherry tomatoes!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups pasta
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1 dash garlic salt
  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 3 tablespoon vodka
  • 3/4 cup parmesan

Steps

  1. Cook pasta al dente (8-10 minutes), then drain
  2. Melt butter into large saucepan on low heat, add garlic salt
  3. Increase heat, added in parmesan and stir
  4. Add cooked pasta to sauce, then garnish with tomatoes and spinach
  5. Serve and enjoy!
