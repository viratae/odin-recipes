Spicy Vodka

Pasta that's nice with a little bit of spice!

Rigatoni pasta dressed with a nice, creamy red and mildly spicy vodka sauce. Finished off with parmesan, shaved garlic, basil, and italian sausage, and crispy pancetta!

Ingredients

  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1/3 lb italian sausage
  • 1/3 lb crispy pancetta
  • 1/4 cup basil
  • 3 tablespoon vodka
  • 24 oz rigatoni pasta
  • 1/3 cup whipping cream
  • 8oz grated parmesan

Steps

  1. Heat garlic and sausage over medium high in deep skillet until sausage is cooked
  2. Add fresh basil, crush and add tomatoes, and add 8oz parmesan. Cook for 20 minutes and stir occasionally
  3. Add in vodka and whipping cream for another 30 minutes
  4. Boil and drain pasta to preferred cook
  5. Add the pasta to the sauce for a few minutes, add any preferred spices, and enjoy!
